About AquaLive

Malaysia's trusted online aquarium store — bringing the ocean's beauty to your home safely and ethically.

Our Story

Born from a passion for fishkeeping, built to serve a community

AquaLive was founded by aquarium enthusiasts who were frustrated by the limitations of physical pet stores in Malaysia — inconsistent stock, poor fish health, and zero guidance for beginners.

We set out to build a platform where every fish is health-checked before dispatch, every order is oxygen-packed for safe transit, and every customer receives free expert guidance through our Fish Care Guide.

Today, AquaLive serves hundreds of happy fishkeepers across Peninsular Malaysia — from first-time guppy owners to seasoned Discus collectors.

Delivery Process

Every fish we ship follows this strict 5-step process

1

Order Received

Your order triggers our packing team within 2 hours during business hours.

2

Fish Selection & Health Check

We hand-pick your fish and conduct a final health inspection before packing.

3

Oxygen-Filled Packing

Each fish is sealed in an oxygen-filled polythene bag, placed in an insulated polystyrene box with heat or cool packs depending on season.

4

Express Courier

Dispatched with a tracked express courier. Transit time is typically 12–24 hours for Peninsular Malaysia.

5

Safe Arrival & DOA Guarantee

If any fish arrives dead, submit photo evidence within 24 hours for a free replacement or refund.

📦 Dead on Arrival (DOA) Policy

We guarantee every fish we ship. If your fish arrives dead, photograph it clearly in the unopened bag and contact us within 24 hours of delivery. We will send a free replacement or issue a full refund — no questions asked.

⚠️ DOA claims must include photo evidence within 24 hours of delivery. Claims without evidence or after 24 hours cannot be processed.
